Business Tips for Farmers

Pricing Your Products Competitively

Learn how to set prices that attract customers while ensuring you make a profit. Consider your costs, market prices, and what customers are willing to pay.

Tip: Start by researching what similar products are selling for in your area.

Build Strong Customer Relationships

Your customers are your best marketers. Keep them happy with good service, quick responses, and consistent product quality.

Tip: Always follow up with customers after sales to build trust.
